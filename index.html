About Me

Undergraduate student at IEM Kolkata. I have been a part of the Microsoft Student Partners Program, which is a worldwide recognisable program to sponsor students majoring in disciplines related to technology. Being a part of the program, I have worked with Microsoft Azure, Microsoft Power BI and other Microsoft Technologies. I am also part of Wealthy Waste, a Kolkata Based startup focusing on sustainable development. I handle operations and marketing related roles for the company.

Personally, I have worked on projects in the field of Machine Learning, Data Science, Data Analytics, Natural Language Processing. I am active on Kaggle and have made many contributions to that platform. I also have knowledge of Economics and Finances.
